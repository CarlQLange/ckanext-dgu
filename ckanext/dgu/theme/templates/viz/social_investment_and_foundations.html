    Exploring: Social Investment and Foundations

    Visualising Investment Data
    These visualisations tell the story of the Social Investment and Foundations dataset. The raw data is a simple CSV file; here we use iPython Notebook to explore and extract the data, and D3.js to render a series of graphs.

    If you are interested in the code, the extract-transform-load script is available on the iPython Notebook Viewer.
