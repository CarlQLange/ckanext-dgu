Detail

The Environment Agency works with water companies to minimise the impact that their assets and activities have on the environment. They also work closely with other regulators of the water industry to ensure a joined-up approach.

There are 9 water and sewerage companies that mainly operate in England providing clean (drinking) water and waste water (sewerage) services. There are also 11 water-only companies providing drinking water, and 3 other companies who offer services to a small number of customers in specific locations.

This report is just about the 9 water and sewerage companies in England.
